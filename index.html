Chapter 2: The "Real" Family Planning Movement

.... was the decade before 1979. Since Mao, the government lauched various campaigns promoting family planning for families to have two children or less. Through harsh enforcement measures, THIS was China's actual family planning policy whose effects we see today.

Late marriage and delayed childbearing were heavily promoted to slow down population growth.
The "Wan, Xi, Shao" (Later, Longer, Fewer) campaign actively encouraged a maximum of two children globally.
Local community groups and workers were organized to study family planning policies together.
Barefoot doctors and medical workers actively distributed contraceptives and planning materials to the masses.
